08 — Guidelines
Do & Don't

Do

  • Use background contrast for card separation (no shadows)
  • Keep Epilogue for headings, Sora for everything else
  • Alternate white/cream sections for visual rhythm
  • Use #0E2A46 for all headings consistently
  • Use pill-radius for all buttons and badges
  • Keep gradients minimal — only where specified
  • Use #0077B6 as the single primary action color

Don't

  • Add box-shadows to cards
  • Use Baloo 2 or Roboto on the public site
  • Mix black (#000) with navy (#0E2A46) for headings
  • Use colored borders on cards (bg contrast only)
  • Introduce new colors without adding to this system
  • Use more than 2 font families on a single page
  • Add decorative gradients to section backgrounds
